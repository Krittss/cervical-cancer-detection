About Cervical Cancer

Cervical cancer is one of the most common cancers affecting women worldwide. It develops in the cells of the cervix and is primarily caused by persistent infection with high-risk human papillomavirus (HPV).

Early detection plays a critical role in reducing mortality rates. Traditional screening methods require expert analysis and can be time-consuming.

CerviScan uses Artificial Intelligence to assist in early detection by analyzing cervical cell images and providing quick predictions along with risk levels.

This system helps doctors and patients make faster and more informed decisions.
